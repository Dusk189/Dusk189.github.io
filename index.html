Recipe #1: Pizza

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 1⁄2 cups
Salt 1 teaspoon
Sugar 1 teaspoon
Fast Rise Yeast 1 tablespoon
Water 1 cup
Cooking Oil 1 tablespoon
Toppings
Tomato Sauce 1⁄4 cup
Italian Seasoning 1 teaspoon
Garlic Powder 1⁄2 teaspoon
Salt 1⁄2 teaspoon
Pepper 1⁄8 teaspoon
Pepperoni Slices 1 1⁄2 cups
Shredded Mozzarella
Cheese		 1 cup
Shredded Monterey
Jack Cheese		 1 cup
Grated Parmesan
Cheese		 3 tablespoons

Step 1: In a large bowl,mix the first 4 ingredients.
Step 2: Mix water and oil; add to flour mixture.
Step 3: Turn onto floured surface; knead for 2 minutes.
Step 4: Place in a greased bowl; turning to grease top.
Step 5: Cover and let rise for 20 minutes.
Step 6: Punch down; place on 12in, greased pizza pan.
Step 7: Pat into a circle.
Step 8: Topping: Mix first 5 ingredients and spread over crust.
Step 9: Put a few pepperoni slices on top of sauce.
Step 10: Sprinkle with 1/2 the mozzeralla; 1/2 the monterey jack, and 1/2 the parmesan.
Step 11: Put the rest of the pepperoni on.
Step 12: Repeat the cheese layer.
Step 13:Bake at 400* for 20 minutes or until light brown.

Source: Easy and Quick Homemade Pizza

Recipe #3

  • Time: 22 minutes
  • Serves: 4-6
Ingredients Quantity
10-inch Flour Tortillas 8
Butter 1⁄4 cup
3 Ounce Package
Cream Cheese		 1
Finely Chopped
Green Onion		 1⁄4 cup
Medium Tomatoes
Finely Chopped		 1
Salt 1 Dash
Shredded Colby-Monterey
Jack Cheese		 3 cups

Step 1:Lightly spread one side of each tortilla with butter; place four tortillas buttered side down on large skillet or griddle (may have to cook in batches).
Step 2: Whip cream cheese until fluffy; stir in onions, tomato and salt.
Step 3: Spread on tortillas in skillet to within 1/2-inch of edge; sprinkle on Colby Jack cheese.
Step 4: Top with remaining tortillas, buttered side up.
Step 5: Cook on medium heat until bottom is lightly browned, about 2 minutes.
Step 6: Carefully flip quesadillas over to brown other side.
Step 7: Remove from heat and cut into 4 or 6 wedges.

Source: Cheese Quesadillas
